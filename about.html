About Us

Pamela Santana Group Family Daycare provides a safe, clean, and nurturing environment where children learn through play, creativity, and exploration.

We are licensed by New York State and proudly serve children from 6 weeks to 12 years old.

Our program offers bilingual support in English and Spanish, nutritious meals and snacks, educational activities, and individualized attention to help every child grow and succeed.
